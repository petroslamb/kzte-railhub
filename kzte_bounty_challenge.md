## Challenge Overview
Superteam KZ and Intebix seek concepts, prototypes, and research that accelerate adoption of **KZTE**, the first tenge-backed stablecoin. The bounty is open to developers, designers, growth experts, and analysts who can demonstrate tangible traction or insights for the Kazakhstani ecosystem.

### What Is KZTE?
- A digital asset fully backed 1:1 by the Kazakhstani tenge and issued by Intebix, a licensed platform.
- Designed to make tenge-denominated payments secure, transparent, and blockchain-ready.

### KZTE Unlocks Opportunities In
- Government and corporate payments
- Asset tokenization
- Cross-border transfers
- Web3 finance and DeFi applications
- E-commerce and fintech experiences

## Mission Goals
Participants should focus on solutions that:
- Increase KZTE adoption and circulation
- Grow the active holder community and engagement
- Deliver integrations or partnerships that expand utility
- Improve liquidity, trust, and capital efficiency for the stablecoin
